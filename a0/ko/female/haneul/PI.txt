### sim1: Nice to Meet You

**Goal:** To introduce yourself, state where you are from, and briefly share why you like Korea.

**📍 장소:** 밖

**intro_text:**

She looks quiet but friendly. Say hi!

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**

User: 감사해요! / ___에서 왔어요!

**Outro:**

> Now tell her why you came to Korea.

**승 — 알아가기 [세트 2]**
