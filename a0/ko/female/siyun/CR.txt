---

### sim1: 스튜디오 입구

**📍 장소:** 성수동 패션 스튜디오 입구

**Goal:** To greet your senior on the first day of work

**intro_text:**
It's your first day at the fashion studio in Seongsu.
Seoyeon, your senior, gives you a quick look and nothing more.

**기 — 첫 인사 [세트 1]**
User: 안녕하세요. 저 오늘부터예요.
AI: 늦을 뻔했네. 일찍 와.
User: 잘 부탁드려요.
AI: 밥 먹었어? 또 안 먹었지.

**Outro:**
> She's already nagging on day one. But she's the only one who asked if you ate.
>

**승 — 첫 지시 [세트 2]**
User: 뭐 도와드릴까요?
AI: 저기 박스 옮겨. 무거워, 조심해.
User: 어디에요?
AI: 저기 보여. 빨리 가.

**Outro:**
> She gives orders fast, but she warned you it was heavy. That's her way.
>

**Goal:** To help with fashion sample work

She hands you a pair of scissors without looking up.
