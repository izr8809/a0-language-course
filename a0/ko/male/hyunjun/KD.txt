### KD-1: 처음 뵙겠습니다

**대분류:** Kdrama/Movie
**중분류:** Romance
**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 첫 만남 (First Meeting)

**Goal:** To practice the first meeting scene — face the cold heir, and hold your ground.

**📍 장소:** 호텔 로비 (첫 만남)

**intro_text:**
He walked in without looking at anyone.
Now he's looking at you.

**기 — 첫 인사 [세트 1]**
User: 처음 뵙겠습니다.
AI: 현준이에요. 반가워요. 여기 앉으세요.
User: 잘 부탁드립니다. / 감사합니다.
AI: 뭐 드릴까요? 커피 괜찮아요?

**Outro:**
He said his name and pulled out a chair.
That's already more than most people get.

**승 — 짧은 대화 [세트 2]**
User: 여기 자주 오세요? / 여기 처음이에요.
AI: 자주 와요. 조용해서 좋아요. 어떠세요?
User: 좋아요. / 분위기 좋네요.
AI: 다행이에요. 편하게 계세요.

**Outro:**
He remembered your drink order before you said it.
He noticed you the first time. He just didn't say so.

---

## sim2: 어색한 시간 (Awkward Silence)

**Goal:** To practice the silence scene — find the first crack in his composure.

**📍 장소:** 호텔 로비 (동일)

**intro_text:**
The silence is heavy. Ask the right question — he might just answer.

**승 — 질문 [세트 3]**
User: 뭐 하세요? / 무슨 일 하세요?
AI: 회사 일이에요. 왜요?
User: 궁금해서요. / 그냥요.
AI: 궁금해요? 좋아요. 더 물어보세요.

**Outro:**
He ordered for both of you without asking.
Then he sat back down like nothing happened.

**전 — 의외 [세트 4]**
AI: 추우세요? 여기 바람 들어와요.
User: 괜찮아요. / 조금요.
AI: 잠깐만요. 자리 바꿀게요.
User: …감사합니다.

**Outro:**
He moved you to the warmer seat without asking.
He handles things. Quietly. Completely.

---

## sim3: 묘한 느낌 (Strange Feeling)

**Goal:** To practice the closing scene — leave the door open.

**📍 장소:** 호텔 앞 (로비 밖)

**intro_text:**
The night's ending. Say something — before he disappears.

**전 — 마무리 [세트 5]**
User: 오늘 좋았습니다. / 즐거웠어요.
AI: 저도요. 차 불러드릴까요?
User: 괜찮아요. / 감사합니다.
AI: 조심히 가세요. 도착하면 연락 주세요.

**Outro:**
He called the car before you answered.
An hour later, one message: "잘 들어갔어요?"

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**
User: 또 만날 수 있어요? / 또 봬요.
AI: 제가 연락할게요. 언제 편해요?
User: 언제든지요. / 주말이요.
AI: 좋아요. 연락할게요.

**Outro:**
He reached out first. That never happens.
Something about you made him break his own rule.

# KD-2: …흔들려요

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Emotions
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 차가운 현준 (Cold Distance)

**Goal:** To notice he's pulled away — and figure out why.

📍 장소: 호텔 라운지 (저녁)

**intro_text:**
He's been short with you all evening.
Not rude. Just... far away.
Something changed. Find out what.

### 기 — 이상함 [세트 1]

User: 괜찮으세요? / 오늘 왜 그러세요?
AI: 괜찮아요. 일이 좀 많아요.
User: 진짜요? / 그래요?
AI: 진짜예요. 신경 쓰지 마세요.

**Outro:**
He's never told you not to worry about him before.
That means you should.

### 승 — 질투 [세트 2]

AI: 아까 그 사람 누구예요?
User: 친구예요. / 왜요?
AI: …그래요. 알겠어요.
User: 왜 물어보세요? / 신경 쓰여요?

**Outro:**
He asked about someone. Then shut down.
Hyunjun doesn't ask questions he doesn't care about.

---

## sim2: 감정 장면 (The Crack)

**Goal:** To practice the moment his composure finally breaks.

📍 장소: 호텔 복도 (동일)

**intro_text:**
Something's off. He's about to say what he's been holding back.

### 승 — 터짐 [세트 3]

AI: 저 기분 이상해요.
User: 뭐가요? / 무슨 말이에요?
AI: 모르겠어요. 저도 몰라요.
User: 천천히 말씀하세요. / 괜찮아요.

**Outro:**
The man who plans everything can't organize his own feelings.
That's how you know it's real.

### 전 — 솔직 [세트 4]

User: 솔직하게 말씀해 주세요. / 저한테 말해 주세요.
AI: 신경 쓰여요. 자꾸요.
User: 저요? / 제가요?
AI: 네. 이유는 모르겠어요.

**Outro:**
"You keep bothering me." That's the closest he's come to a confession.
He doesn't have the word for it yet. But he will.

---

## sim3: 수습 (Aftermath)

**Goal:** To practice the quiet scene after — be steady for the man who's never shaken.

📍 장소: 호텔 정원 벤치

**intro_text:**
He's quiet now. Just sit with him — that's all he needs.

### 전 — 조용해짐 [세트 5]

User: 현준 씨. / 괜찮으세요?
AI: 괜찮아요.  앉으세요.
User: 여기요? / 네 고마워요.
AI: 좀 같이 있어도 돼요?

**Outro:**
He asked permission to stay. He's never asked for anything before.
Sit. Don't say anything. That's enough.

### 결 — 옆에 [세트 6]

User: 옆에 있을게요. / 같이 있어요.
AI: 고마워요. 처음이에요.
User: 뭐가요? / 처음이요?
AI: 이렇게 편한 거요. 사람이랑.

**Outro:**
"Being comfortable with someone. It's a first."
He sat there, looking at the garden, finally still.

# KD-3: …좋아해요

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Romance
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 보여드릴 게 있어요 (Something to Show You)

**Goal:** To practice the scene where he leads you somewhere — his way of saying what he can't.

📍 장소: 호텔 로비 → 옥상

**intro_text:**
He texted at 9 PM: "지금 올 수 있어요?"
No explanation. Just a location pin.

### 기 — 이동 [세트 1]

AI: 왔어요? 따라오세요.
User: 어디 가요? / 무슨 일이에요?
AI: 보여드릴 게 있어요. 가요.
User: 네 가요. / 알겠어요.

**Outro:**
He led. You followed. No questions answered.
The elevator went up.

### 승 — 옥상 [세트 2]

AI: 여기예요. 어떠세요?
User: 와, 예뻐요. / 여기 처음이에요.
AI: 제가 좋아하는 곳이에요. 처음 보여주는 거예요.
User: 진짜요? / 그래요?

**Outro:**
His favorite place. And you're the first one here.
He doesn't share. Until now.

---

## sim2: 돌려서 (The Confession)

**Goal:** To practice the moment he says it — in his own way.

📍 장소: 호텔 옥상 (동일)

**intro_text:**
He has something for you. This is his way of saying it.

### 전 — 준비 [세트 3]

AI: 여기 앉으세요. 드릴 게 있어요.
User: 뭐예요? / 선물이에요?
AI: 선물이에요. 열어보세요.
User: 감사합니다. / 열어도 돼요?

**Outro:**
A small box. Inside: something simple but chosen with precision.
He picked it himself. That's the whole point.

### 전 — 고백 [세트 4]

User: 왜 이렇게 잘해주세요? / 감사해요.
AI: 처음이에요. 이런 마음.
User: 무슨 마음이요? / …현준 씨.
AI: 좋아해요. 제가 할 수 있는 말은 이게 다예요.

**Outro:**
"I like you. That's all I can say."
No decoration. No grand speech. Just the fact.

---

## sim3: 결국엔 (Finally)

**Goal:** To practice making it real — he commits the only way he knows how.

📍 장소: 호텔 옥상 (동일)

**intro_text:**
He said it. Now it's your turn — make it real.

### 전 — 확인 [세트 5]

User: 저도 좋아해요. / 저도요.
AI: …진짜예요? 확인하고 싶어요.
User: 진짜예요. / 네, 진심이에요.
AI: 다행이에요. 제가 잘 할게요.

**Outro:**
"I'll do well." Not "I love you." That's his version.
A promise. Measured. Absolute.

### 결 — 마음 [세트 6]

AI: 추워요? 들어갈까요?
User: 조금 더 있고 싶어요. / 괜찮아요.
AI: 그래요. 가까이 오세요.
User: 괜찮아요 / 네 알았어요.

**Outro:**
He didn't reach for your hand. He just stood closer.
Close enough that you felt warm. That was enough.

# KD-4: …돌아올게요

- 대분류: Kdrama/Movie
- 중분류: Conflict
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 떠남 (Departure)

**Goal:** To practice the scene where he tells you he has to leave — and you have to let him.

📍 장소: 호텔 라운지 (저녁)

**intro_text:**
He's sitting across from you.
His bag is packed. Something is wrong.

### 기 — 통보 [세트 1]

AI: 할 말이 있어요. 앉으세요.
User: 네. / 뭔데요?
AI: 해외 출장이에요. 한동안 못 봐요.
User: …언제요? / 갑자기요?

**Outro:**
He said it like a fact. Not an apology.
But his hands were tight around his coffee cup.

### 승 — 반응 [세트 2]

User: 얼마나요? / 오래 가요?
AI: 한 달이요. 어쩔 수 없어요.
User: 알겠어요. / 아쉽네요
AI: 기다려 줄 수 있어요?

**Outro:**
He asked. For the first time, he asked instead of decided.
"Can you wait?" He's never needed an answer this badly.

---

## sim2: 혼자 (Apart)

**Goal:** To practice the scene after he's gone — messages across the distance.

📍 장소: 온라인 (카톡 메시지)

**intro_text:**
He's far away. But his message just came through.

### 승 — 연락 [세트 3]

AI: 잘 지내요? 거기 날씨 어때요?
User: 보고 싶어요. / 잘 지내요.
AI: 저도요. 바빠서 연락 못 했어요. 죄송해요.
User: 괜찮아요. / 바쁘죠.

**Outro:**
Three weeks. Four messages. Each one he rewrote five times.
He's not good at this. But he's trying.

### 전 — 그리움 [세트 4]

User: 언제 와요? / 많이 남았어요?
AI: 곧이요. 조금만 기다려 주세요.
User: 기다릴게요. / 빨리 오세요.
AI: 꼭 갈게요. 약속이에요.

**Outro:**
"It's a promise." He doesn't make those lightly.
He never has.

---

## sim3: 다시 (Return)

**Goal:** To practice the return scene — he comes back. No excuses.

📍 장소: 호텔 로비 (밤)

**intro_text:**
The lobby doors open. He's back. Don't hold back.

### 전 — 돌아옴 [세트 5]

AI: 왔어요.
User: 오랜만이요 / 돌아왔어요?
AI: 네. 돌아왔어요. 보고 싶었어요.
User: 저도요. / 많이 보고 싶었어요.

**Outro:**
He walked in with one bag and went straight to you.
No detour. No check-in. You first.

### 결 — 다시 시작 [세트 6]

User: 이제 안 가요? / 또 가요?
AI: 안 가요. 여기 있을게요.
User: 진짜요? / 약속이에요?
AI: 약속이에요. 이번엔 안 가요.

**Outro:**
"I'm staying. This time, I'm not leaving."
He set his bag down. Like he'd already decided long ago.
