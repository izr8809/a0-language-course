# KP-1: 왔어요?

- 대분류: Kpop
- 중분류: Fan Meeting
- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: First Sign

**Goal:** To stand in front of Hyunjun at the fan sign and get through the moment without freezing.

**📍 장소:** 팬미팅 사인회 (테이블)

**intro_text:**
He's already seated.
Pen in hand. Eyes down.
You're next in line.

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
User: 안녕하세요! / 반갑습니다!
AI: 안녕하세요. 반가워요.
User: 반가워요! / 저도요!
AI: 여기 앉으세요.

**Outro:**
> He smiled — barely. And pulled out the chair himself.
> Ask before the moment passes.
>

**승 — 사인 [세트 2]**
User: 사인 해주세요! / 사인 부탁해요!
AI: 이름 알려주세요.
User: ___이에요!
AI: 좋아요. 써줄게요.

**Outro:**
> He wrote it slowly.
> He didn't have to. He did.
>
