Deep breath... say hi!

## sim1: Nice to Meet You

**Goal:** To introduce yourself, state where you are from, and briefly share why you like Korea.

**📍 장소:** 밖

**intro_text:**
First meeting in Korea. Deep breath!

**기 — 첫 만남 [세트 1]**
AI: 안녕하세요. 현준이에요. 이름이 뭐예요?

AI: 반가워요. 어디서 왔어요?
User: ___에서 왔어요!

**Outro:**
He said your name back. Quietly. Filed away.

**승 — 알아가기 [세트 2]**

**Outro:**
Short question. But he actually listened.
