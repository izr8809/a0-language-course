### KD-1: 나 한결이에요!

**대분류:** Kdrama/Movie
**중분류:** Romance
**난이도:** A0 (Absolute Beginner)
**구성:** 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 첫 만남 (First Meeting)

**Goal:** To practice the first meeting scene — when he crashes into your world with full energy.

**📍 장소:** 건대 캠퍼스 (첫 만남)

**intro_text:**
New semester. You're barely awake.
Then — a voice. Loud. Bright. Impossible to ignore.

**기 — 첫 인사 [세트 1]**
AI: 안녕하세요! 나 한결이에요!
User: 아, 네. 반가워요.
AI: 이름이 뭐예요?
User: ___이에요!

**Outro:**
> He asked your name before you even sat down. 😄
> That's just how he is.
>

**승 — 반가워요 [세트 2]**
AI: 같은 수업이에요? 진짜요?
User: 네! / 그런 것 같아요.
AI: 대박! 잘 부탁해요!
User: 저도 잘 부탁해요!

**Outro:**
> He said it like you've been friends for years. 🌟
> You haven't. But somehow it feels that way.
>

- 난이도: A0 (Absolute Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: 다퉜어 (We Fought)

**Goal:** To practice the fight scene — when even he goes quiet.

📍 장소: 건대 공원 (밤)

**intro_text:**
He said something wrong.
He knows it. You both do.

### 기 — 말다툼 [세트 1]

User: 그냥 가.
AI: 미안해요. 진짜 미안해요.
User: 가!
AI: 가고 싶지 않아요.

**Outro:**
> He apologized first. Immediately. 😶
> He hates fighting with you.
>

### 승 — 이별 [세트 2]

User: 그만하자.
AI: 싫어요. 그러지 마요.
User: 한결 씨.
AI: 한 번만 기회 줘요. 잘할게요.

**Outro:**
> He said no. Then asked for one more chance. 💔
> He's not giving up that easily.
>
