# PI-5: 咱们去吃饭吧！

- 대분류: Personal Interest
- 중분류: Hanging Out
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: 选地方

**Goal:** To suggest a meal idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**📍 장소:** 三里屯街头

**intro_text:**
Lunchtime in Beijing. Endless options. What are we eating?

**기 — 吃什么 [세트 1]**
User: 我饿了！吃什么好？
AI: 我也饿了！你想吃什么？
User: 想吃烤肉！/ 吃烤肉怎么样？
AI: 好啊！我知道一家超好吃的！走吧！

**Outro:**
> Kǎoròu it is! 🥩 Now, how far is this place?
> Your stomach is already counting down.
>

**승 — 怎么去 [세트 2]**
AI: 就在附近！走路去吧！
User: 好！要走多久？/ 走路去啊？
AI: 五分钟就到了！走吧！
User: 好的！

**Outro:**
> Five minutes away! 🚶 The smell of grilling meat is basically pulling you there.
> Almost there — let's go!
