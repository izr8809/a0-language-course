**Goal:** To reconnect, share what you've been up to lately, and talk about everyday life.

# PI-2: 우리 밥 먹으러 가자

- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

**Goal:** To suggest a food idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**intro_text:**

User: 삼겹살 먹고 싶어! / 삼겹살 어때?
AI: 좋아. 맛있는 데 알아. 가자.

**Outro:**

> Even picking food feels calm with Taeo.
>

**승 — 가는 길 [세트 2]**

User: 삼겹살 2인분 / 된장찌개!

**Outro:**

>

**전 — 먹기 [세트 4]**

User: 진짜 맛있다! / 여기 잘 찾았다!

**Outro:**

> Taeo smiles softly. Time to wrap up.
>

---

## sim3: After Eating

**Goal:** To insist on paying the bill and make plans to keep the hangout going.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안 → 식당 밖 길거리

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**

**Outro:**
