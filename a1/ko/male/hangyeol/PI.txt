# PI-1: 같이 밥 먹을래요?

- 대분류: Personal Interest
- 중분류: Hanging Out
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: Choosing a Place

**Goal:** To suggest a food idea and agree on a spot together — still in 존댓말, warming up.

**📍 장소:** 길거리

**intro_text:**
Lunch time in Korea. You've hung out a few times now, but you're still speaking politely.
So many options... what should we eat?

**기 — 뭐 먹을까요 [세트 1]**
AI: 배고프지 않아요? 우리 밥 먹을까요?
User: 네, 배고파요! 빨리 먹고 싶어요! / 좋아요! 뭐 먹을까요?
AI: 삼겹살 좋아해요? 맛있는 식당 알아요!
User: 네, 삼겹살 좋아해요! / 어디에 있어요?

**Outro:**
> Samgyeopsal it is! He's already jumping up and down — classic Hangyeol.
> He's dragging you by the arm before you even finish saying yes!
>

**승 — 가는 길 [세트 2]**
AI: 식당이 여기에서 가까워요. 지금 가요!
User: 얼마나 걸려요?
AI: 오 분 걸려요!
User: 기대돼요!

**Outro:**
> He's already sprinting ahead! You can barely keep up!
> Almost there — he's screaming about the meat smell from half a block away.
