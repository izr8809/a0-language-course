# PI-2: 우리 밥 먹으러 가자

- 대분류: Personal Interest
- 중분류: Hanging Out
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

**Goal:** To suggest a food idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**📍 장소:** 길거리

**intro_text:**

**기 — 뭐 먹을까 [세트 1]**

AI: 좋아요. 뭐 먹고 싶어요?
User: 삼겹살 먹고 싶어요! / 삼겹살 어때요?
AI: 좋아요. 제가 식당을 알아요.

**Outro:**
> She nodded quietly. Samgyeopsal — warm and simple, just right.
> Follow her lead. She knows the way.
>

**승 — 가는 길 [세트 2]**
User: 가까워요? / 얼마나 걸려요?

AI: 네, 빨리 가요.

**Outro:**

## sim2: At the Restaurant

**Goal:** To order food together and bond over grilling meat like a true Korean local.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안

**intro_text:**
We're here. This place looks amazing.

**승 — 식당 [세트 3]**
AI: 여기예요.
User: 와, 식당이 예뻐요!
AI: 메뉴판 여기 있어요. 뭐 먹을까요?
User: 삼겹살이랑 된장찌개요! / 삼겹살이요!

**Outro:**
> She picked the window seat without a word. Light came in just right.
> Order placed. Now the quiet ritual of grilling begins.
>

**전 — 먹기 [세트 4]**
AI: 제가 고기 구워 줄게요.
User: 고마워요! / 정말요? 좋아요!
AI: 맛있어요?
User: 진짜 맛있어요! / 최고예요!

**Outro:**
> She ate slowly, savoring each bite. The warm light suited the moment.
> Now comes the bill — handle it gently.
>

---

## sim3: After Eating

**Goal:** To insist on paying the bill and make plans to keep the hangout going.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안 → 식당 밖 길거리

**전 — 식사 후 [세트 5]**

AI: 고마워요. 다음엔 제가 살게요.

**Outro:**
> She accepted quietly, with a small nod. That's how she says thank you.
> The afternoon still has room. Where to next?
>

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**
User: 좋아요! 산책할까요? / 배불러요. 걷고 싶어요!
AI: 네, 커피 마시면서 걸어요.
User: 좋아요!
AI: 저기 예쁜 길로 가요.

**Outro:**
> Full and warm. She looked up at the sky before stepping outside.
> A slow walk together — the best kind of ending.
