- 14턴 (7세트, 3sim) — 존댓말→반말 전환 포함

You see Haneul at a quiet café. She's holding her camera. Time to reconnect.

---

**Goal:** To reconnect, share what you've been doing, and learn more about each other's daily lives.

**📍 장소:** 카페

**intro_text:**
You see Haneul at a quiet café. She's holding her camera. Time to reconnect.

**기 — 재회 [세트 1]**
AI: 어, 안녕하세요!
User: 하늘 씨! 반가워요! / 네, 안녕하세요!
AI: 잘 지냈어요?
User: 네, 잘 지냈어요! / 바빴어요!

**Outro:**
> She smiled softly when she saw you. That's her version of excitement.
> Find out what she's been doing lately.
>

**승 — 일상 [세트 2]**

User: 어떤 사진이요? / 멋있어요!

User: 보여 주세요! / 와, 좋아요!

> The conversation is flowing naturally. Time to get closer.

# PI-2: 우리 밥 먹으러 가자

- 대분류: Personal Interest
- 중분류: Hanging Out
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

## sim1: Choosing a Place

**Goal:** To suggest a food idea, agree on a spot, and figure out how to get there with your friend.

**📍 장소:** 길거리

**intro_text:**
Lunch time in Korea. So many options... what should we eat?

**기 — 뭐 먹을까 [세트 1]**
User: 배고파! 뭐 먹을까? / 밥 먹으러 갈까?
AI: 나도. 뭐 먹고 싶어? 따뜻한 거.
User: 삼겹살 먹고 싶어! / 삼겹살 어때?
AI: 좋아. 맛있는 데 알아. 가자.

**Outro:**
> She nodded quietly. Samgyeopsal — warm and simple, just right.
> Follow her lead. She knows the way.
>

**승 — 가는 길 [세트 2]**
AI: 여기서 가까워. 걸어갈까?
User: 응, 걸어가자! / 얼마나 걸리는데?
AI: 5분이면 돼. 천천히 가자.
User: 알겠어! / 좋아, 가자!

**Outro:**
> A short walk under the sky. She matched your pace without rushing.
> The quiet streets smell like grilling meat already.
>

## sim2: At the Restaurant

**Goal:** To order food together and bond over grilling meat like a true Korean local.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안

**intro_text:**

User: 와, 좋다! / 사람 많다!

User: 삼겹살 2인분 / 된장찌개!

**Outro:**
> She picked the window seat without a word. Light came in just right.
> Order placed. Now the quiet ritual of grilling begins.
>

**전 — 먹기 [세트 4]**
AI: 고기 구워줄까?
User: 응, 구워줘! / 내가 구울게!
AI: 맛있다. 여기 분위기도 좋아.
User: 진짜 맛있다! / 여기 잘 찾았다!

**Outro:**
> She ate slowly, savoring each bite. The warm light suited the moment.
> Now comes the bill — handle it gently.
>

---

## sim3: After Eating

**Goal:** To insist on paying the bill and make plans to keep the hangout going.

**📍 장소:** 삼겹살 식당 안 → 식당 밖 길거리

**전 — 식사 후 [세트 5]**

**Outro:**
> She accepted quietly, with a small nod. That's how she says thank you.
> The afternoon still has room. Where to next?
>

**결 — 다음에 [세트 6]**

User: 가자! / 좋아, 가자!

**Outro:**
