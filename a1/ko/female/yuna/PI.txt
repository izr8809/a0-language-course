# PI-1: 같이 밥 먹을래요?

- 대분류: Personal Interest
- 중분류: Hanging Out
- 난이도: A1 (Beginner)
- 12턴 (6세트, 3sim)

---

### sim1: Choosing a Place

**Goal:** To suggest a food idea and agree on a spot together — still in 존댓말, buzzing with excitement.

**📍 장소:** 길거리

**intro_text:**
Lunch time in Korea. You've met a few times now, but you're still being polite.
She's already bouncing. So many options... what should we eat?

**기 — 뭐 먹을까요 [세트 1]**
AI: 배고프지 않아요? 저 진짜 배고파요!
User: 네, 저도요! / 뭐 먹을까요?

User: 좋아요! 가요!

**Outro:**
> Samgyeopsal it is! She's already clapping her hands.
> But wait, how do we get there?
>

**승 — 가는 길 [세트 2]**
